Who TF Are Seedhe Maut?

Short answer: Delhi’s deadliest duo.
Long answer: scroll—made just for you. 💙

1) Who tf are Seedhe Maut?!

They’re a two-man rap superteam: Encore ABJ (Abhijay Negi) + Calm (Sidhant Sharma) out of Delhi. Not “Seedhe” and “Maut” as two people—together they’re Seedhe Maut.

“Best in the business.” — especially according to my favorite fan: you.

2) What do they do?

They rap—hard. Hinglish bars, Delhi slang, and stories that hit: personal grind, city life, ambition, and real talk. Wordplay that makes you rewind, flows that switch mid-verse, and beats (often by Sez on the Beat) that slap in clubs and in headphones.

“Seedhe Maut ke bina kya jeevan hai?” — you, probably, every day.

Calm brings the deep, poetic weight; ABJ brings the razor-sharp technicals. Together? Yin-yang energy. One second smooth, next second flamethrower—still crystal clear.

3) Why are they so famous?

Because they’re hasty flows + insane rhyme schemes + wild switch-ups wrapped in realness. No Bollywood sellout vibe—just pure hip-hop. They speak hustle, heartbreak, headbangers, and heavy thoughts. Whether you’re grinding, raging, or overthinking, there’s a track for that.

Calm = depth & poetry · ABJ = raw & surgical · Together = chaos you can’t skip.

From underground cyphers to festival chaos, they turned stages into riots and listeners into lifers. Fans across languages still vibe—even if they don’t speak Hindi. The energy translates.

4) Quick story (like your playlist: no skips)

Two Delhi boys orbit the same scene—bars for days, chemistry on sight—Seedhe Maut forms.

2017: 2 Ka Pahada drops. Warning shot heard round DHH.
2018: Bayaan with Azadi Records—momentum unlocked.
2022: Nayaab era—no more “up next”; they’re the ones.

Not just flex—real talk about Delhi, dreams, and doing the hard things. Hinglish storytelling + heavyweight production = signature SM.
